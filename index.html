RAG Incident Resolution Engine

Problem

The operations team was spending ~45 minutes per incident manually reading through hundreds of Runbooks to find the right resolution steps — during live production failures where every minute matters.

Solution

Built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline on Databricks. Embedded 200+ historical Runbooks and RCA documents into a vector store. During live failures, the pipeline semantically retrieves the top matching resolution steps and presents them automatically.

Additional Integration

LLM-generated incident summaries from Splunk log dumps are automatically injected into ServiceNow ticket creation — eliminating manual log-reading and accelerating stakeholder communication.

Result

Triage time: 45 minutes → under 10 minutes. 40% improvement in incident resolution speed.

Result

40% reduction in data quality incidents reaching BI consumers.
